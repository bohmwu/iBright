LED Principal

LED is the use of electrical energy into light energy, the voltage applied to the anode and cathode of the semiconductor, when the current went through the combination of electron and hole, the remaining energy will be released in the form of light, depending on the different material, the photon energy produce different wavelengths of light, so the human eye can receive different colors of light.

LED Advantage

Various sizes and colors, highly rigid and reliable, long life, about 50,000 to 100,000 hours;

Low power consumption, low intensity decay rate, low heat;

Available in high intensity, high voltage and high power version, high purity of color;

Low Reaction time - 1uS, no need to warm up.
